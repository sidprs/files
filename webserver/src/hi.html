Frequently asked questions

What is hosting, and why do I need it?

For a website to be accessible online, the website’s files must be stored on a server. This is where web hosting comes in.

In very simple terms, a hosting service is what allows you to make your website visible on the Internet so that anyone can visit it.

The domain, on the other hand, is the “name” that people enter in the address bar to reach your website.

What types of hosting do you offer?

We have solutions for all your needs. If you want to create your first website and have no special requirements, you can start with shared hosting or WordPress hosting.

For eCommerce we have tailored plans for WooCommerce, PrestaShop and Magento.

If you need a tailored solution, you can choose a cloud VPS hosting or a dedicated server.

Is SupportHost a good choice for WordPress hosting?

If you want to create a WordPress website easily and minimize management, we have a managed WordPress hosting. Get optimized WordPress service and support tailored to your needs.

How can I transfer domain and website to SupportHost?

If you already have a website with another provider and want to transfer it to SupportHost, we can transfer it for free. Your website’s migration will be handled completely by our experts, and your website will be always online throughout the process.

Zero stress, zero downtime!

All hosting accounts include an email service that allows you to create as many professional email accounts as you want associated with your domain (@domain.com).
